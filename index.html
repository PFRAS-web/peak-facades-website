Quality Consumables for Your Projects

In addition to our facade services, we supply high-quality silicone and glazing consumables. Ensure the longevity and performance of your installations with products you can trust.

Dowsil 791 Weatherproofing Sealant

A one-part, medium-modulus silicone sealant designed for weathersealing applications. It offers excellent weatherability, remaining virtually unaffected by sunlight, rain, snow, and ozone, with ±50% joint movement capability and low odor.

Dowsil 795 Structural Glazing Sealant

A one-component, neutral-curing silicone sealant designed for structural bonding of materials like glass and metal. It's a medium-modulus sealant with strong adhesion to various materials and a movement capability of ±50%.

For inquiries about our consumable products, please contact us directly.
